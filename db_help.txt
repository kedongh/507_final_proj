Parameters:

- [NULL|category], default=NULL
	- The first category within which to limit the results.
- [NULL|location=<city>], default=NULL
	- The city within which to limit the results.
- [rating|review_count|price], default=rating
	- Whether to sort/aggregate by rating, review_count, or price.
- [top|bottom], default=top
	- List results in descending (top) or ascending (bottom) order.
- <integer>, default=10
	- List <limit> matches.
